Our Story

Tasha's Kitchen was founded in 2010 by Natasha Smith, a South African chef with a passion for sharing her country's cuisine with the world. After years of working in some of the country's top restaurants, Natasha decided to strike out on her own and open a restaurant that would showcase the best of South African food and hospitality.

Today, Tasha's Kitchen is a beloved fixture of the local food scene, known for its warm and welcoming atmosphere, attentive service, and delicious food. Whether you're a local or a visitor, we invite you to come and experience the flavors and traditions of South Africa at Tasha's Kitchen.
